About Us!


Let us at Acme use over 30 years of experience to help you with all of your remodeling needs, from minor repairs to major renovations. Acme General Contracting is a multifaceted organization providing a wide range of services for residential, commercial and multihousing customers. Acme is centrally located in Hopkins, Minnesota, only 10 minutes southwest of Downtown Minneapolis, conveniently serving the entire Twin Cities Metropolitan area as well as Greater Minnesota and the entire 5 State Area. We offer Complete, Top Quality, Interior and Exterior Painting, Remodeling and Restoration. No job is too large or too small. We are proud of our professionalism, quality workmanship and our experienced staff. We are your resource for affordable solutions to every project and budget. Our history includes renovating over 50 single family homes for the Metropolitan Council, remodeling hundreds of apartments for local and national property management companies, and remodeling many commercial spaces. We are also the approved representative for the Home Depot Home Painting Services in the Twin Cities area. Acme General Contracting is licensed with the State of Minnesota as a Residential Building Contractor, License # 20265224, and we are fully bonded and insured.
